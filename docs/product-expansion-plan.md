# Product Expansion Plan

Do not publish 1,000 products until every item has supplier cost, shipping cost/time, return rules, image rights, and compliance reviewed.

Current best expansion categories for Bought It Online:

1. Home organization
2. Cleaning tools
3. Kitchen helpers
4. Charging and cable accessories
5. Desk and office utilities
6. Travel organizers
7. Outdoor and camping accessories
8. Fitness and recovery accessories
9. Kids learning tools
10. Pet-safe non-medical accessories
11. Seasonal gifts
12. Low-cost giftable gadgets

Rules for importing products:

- Keep risky health, branded luxury, streaming/copyright, and automation/clicker products in draft.
- Target retail prices under $30 where possible.
- Require at least 45 percent gross margin after product cost, supplier shipping, payment fees, and expected refund allowance.
- Use SEO titles under 70 characters.
- Use plain product names, not supplier keyword spam.
- Start with 50 to 100 verified products, test conversion, then scale.

Recommended free Shopify apps to install manually after permission review:

- Shopify Search & Discovery: filters, recommendations, search relevance.
- Shopify Forms: email/SMS lead capture.
- Shopify Email: basic email campaigns.
- Shopify Inbox: live chat/customer support.
- Shopify Product Reviews alternative such as Judge.me Product Reviews free plan, if acceptable.
- Hextom Free Shipping Bar free plan, if acceptable.
- Shopify Order Printer: packing slips and invoices.
- Digital Products by Shopify: useful if selling downloadable gift content later.

Gift card blocker:

The Admin API supports gift-card products, but Shopify returned: "Gift card products can only be created after they have been activated." Enable native gift cards in Shopify admin first, then rerun the gift-card creation script.
